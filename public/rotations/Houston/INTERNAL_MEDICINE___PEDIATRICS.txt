Description of the Rotation

This is a very good Rotation for people who want to do just Internal medicine OR just pediatric rotation OR a family practice rotation as this contains elements of both adult patients, children, adolescents, newborn. It also involves vaccination, developmental milestones/growth, and other pediatric problems. It also includes adult problems of chronic conditions, like hypertension, diabetes, chronic obstructive, pulmonary disease, etc.
