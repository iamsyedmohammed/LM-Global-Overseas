Description of the Rotation

Jackson Park Hospital caters to a diverse population of America including Caucasians, Blacks, Hispanics, Asians, indigenous, and inner-city populations. The experience is phenomenal. The pathology seen is diverse and workup and diagnostics/Rx the state of the art.

Daily Schedule

Hospital rounds daily in the morning. Timings roughly Monday – Friday: 9 a.m. to 2 p.m. If students are interested, they can do Saturday hours as well. Times are subject to change as per the attending’s schedule.

Outpatient Clinics

Outpatient clinics 1-2 times a week for roughly 4 hours.

Student Responsibilities

Rotating Medical Students at JPH will work closely with the Residents, Fellows, and the attending team in close coordination with each other.

Students may also be required to attend didactics, lectures, and grand rounds at the JPH. While participating as a member of the team, you will learn to interact with diverse patients and perform detailed history taking and physical examination, check labs and notes on the patient. Formulate a differential diagnosis and an assessment and treatment plan for each patient under the attending physician’s supervision and guidance. The students will follow up on all patients under their care every day. They will also participate in discharge planning and execution.

Document Needed

• MMR • PPD (within 6 months) • Proof of Covid vaccine • Immunization records • Resume • Casual business attire or scrubs/Lab coat in hospital and clinics.

Refund Policy

Full payment is due 6 weeks before the rotation start date. You can also pay the entire rotation fee at the start without putting a deposit down. 50% of the amount will be refunded if rotation is canceled before 4 weeks of the start date. No refunds after that.
