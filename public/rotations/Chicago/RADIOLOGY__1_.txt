Description of the Rotation

Rotation in diagnostic and interventional radiology. Candidates will be exposed to the assessment of diagnostic radiology AND WILL BE TAUGHT THE IMAGING MODALITIES OF X-RAYS, CT scans, MRIs, AND OTHERS. The rotation would be for four weeks. 9 AM to 3 PM Monday to Friday. In addition to pursuing diagnostic and interventional radiology, the candidates can approach mentors for research in radiology as well.
