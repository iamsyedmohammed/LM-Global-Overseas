Description of the Rotation

Jackson Park Hospital caters to a diverse population of America including Caucasians, Blacks, Hispanics, Asians, indigenous, and inner-city populations. The experience is phenomenal. The pathology seen is diverse and workup and diagnostics/Rx the state of the art.

Daily Schedule

Hospital rounds daily in the morning PRE OPERATIVE ROUNDS ON THE FLOOR. Timings roughly:

Monday – Friday: FOR THE OR - 9 a.m. to 2 p.m.
Saturday: If students are interested, they can do Saturday hours as well.
On-Call: Once a week they have a choice to remain on call as well with the team on the floor.

Times are subject to change as per the attending’s schedule.

Outpatient Clinics

Outpatient clinics 1-2 times a week for roughly 4 hours.

Responsibilities of Rotating Medical Students

Rotating Medical Students at JPH will work closely with the Residents, Fellows, and the attending team in close coordination with each other. Students may also be required to attend didactics, lectures, and grand rounds at the JPH. While participating as a member of the team, you will:

Learn to interact with diverse patients and perform detailed history taking and physical examination.
Check labs and notes on the patient.
Formulate a differential diagnosis and an assessment and treatment plan for each patient under the attending physician’s supervision and guidance.
Follow up on all patients under their care every day.
Participate in discharge planning and execution.
Document Needed

• MMR • PPD (within 6 months) • Proof of Covid vaccine • Immunization records • Resume • Casual business attire or scrubs/Lab coat in hospital and clinics.
