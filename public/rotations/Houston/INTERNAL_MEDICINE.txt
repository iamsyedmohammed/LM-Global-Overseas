Description of the Rotation

Hospital has a residency program

During your rotation you will have an interaction with teaching attendings, other students, residence and fellows rotating through the program.

Schedule would be both inpatient and outpatient and will be decided by the Attending Physician in advance.

In the clinic the students will have a total hands-on experience involving history taking and physical examination, doing the notes in the electronic medical record EMR, forming an assessment and plan and discussing with the Attending Physician.

The rough time of attendance would be 9 to 10 AM in the morning till 4-5 PM in the evening. The final schedule and timing will be decided by the Attending Physician, some days may be longer and some days may be shorter.

Very good clinical experience of inpatient hospital management and outpatient medical care.

Letter of recommendation LOR on the Hospital letterhead by the teaching faculty and Attending Physician.

LOR will be performance based and individualized.
