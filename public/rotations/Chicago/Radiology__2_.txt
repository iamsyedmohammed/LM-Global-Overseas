Description of the Rotation

Rotation in diagnostic and interventional radiology. Candidates will be exposed to the assessment of diagnostic radiology AND WILL BE TAUGHT THE IMAGING MODALITIES OF X-RAYS, CT scans, MRIs, AND OTHERS. Interventional Radiology procedures for peripheral vascular arterial diseases involving stenting and dilatations, Central Access, catheters, Artery embolization etc. The rotation would be for four weeks. 9 AM to 3 PM Monday to Friday. In addition to pursuing diagnostic and interventional radiology, the candidates can approach mentors for research in radiology as well. The rotation will be at the Hospital and in Out patient Radiology Centers too.
