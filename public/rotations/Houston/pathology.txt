Description of the Rotation

Students will work with the Director of pathology every day from 9 a.m. to 4 p.m. and will evaluate hematology samples, slides, and peripheral smears. They will also analyze the Histopathology slides of the hospital daily. Analysis of Frozen sections from the OR. Autopsy and Forensics attendance as and when needed.
